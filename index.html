Abstract
This paper systematically describes the structure of linear combinations in the group ringCG from three perspectives:algebra,analysis,and quantum mechanics.By realizing the group ring as bounded operators on a Hilbert space through the left regular representation, the study elucidates its spectral properties and trace structures. Notably, it demonstrates that the product in a group ring is isomorphic to the composition of quantum transition amplitudes and reveals that the geometric characteristics of the group are reflected in the spectral radius of the operators.
